OUR STORY

A Legacy Of Inclusion & Quality Care

Method Child Development Center began in 1969 as Wake County's first multi-racial child care center. Founded by five local churches, the Center was created to provide quality early childhood education for children from all socioeconomic backgrounds. Today, Method continues that mission as a nonprofit organization serving infants through school-age children in a welcoming and inclusive environment.

Founded In 1969

Established through the Raleigh Inter-Church Housing Corporation to provide affordable child care for families across the community.

Inclusive Community

Method has proudly welcomed children from diverse backgrounds for more than five decades while celebrating every family's uniqueness.

Five-Star Quality

The Center earned North Carolina's 5-Star License and continues to provide high-quality early childhood education in a safe, supportive environment.

OUR PURPOSE

Our Mission & Vision

For more than five decades, Method Child Development Center has remained committed to providing high-quality early childhood education in a safe, nurturing, inclusive, and affordable environment for every family.

Our Mission

Method Child Development Center supports a diverse community of families by focusing on each child's social, educational, and emotional needs within a safe, nurturing, and affordable environment. As a nonprofit child care center operated by a volunteer Board of Directors, children remain our highest priority.

Our Vision

To inspire confident, compassionate, and curious learners by creating an inclusive community where every child is encouraged to explore, grow, and build a strong foundation for lifelong success.

OUR CURRICULUM

Learning Through Exploration & Discovery

Our curriculum encourages children to learn through meaningful experiences, hands-on exploration, creative play, and positive relationships with teachers and classmates.

Language & Literacy

Developing communication, storytelling, reading readiness, and vocabulary through books, conversations, and classroom activities.

Math & Problem Solving

Children explore numbers, patterns, measurement, counting, and logical thinking through engaging activities.

Science & Discovery

Hands-on experiments and exploration help children understand the world around them through observation and curiosity.

Creative Arts

Art, music, dramatic play, and creative expression encourage imagination and self-confidence.

Outdoor Learning

Outdoor play promotes physical development, teamwork, confidence, and healthy lifestyles through active exploration.

Social & Emotional Growth

Children develop friendships, confidence, kindness, independence, and respect through daily classroom experiences.
