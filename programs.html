DAILY ROUTINE

A Typical Day at Method

Our daily schedule provides children with a healthy balance of learning, creative play, outdoor activities, rest, and social interaction.

7:30 AM

Arrival & Welcome

Children arrive, settle into the classroom, and begin their day with free exploration.

9:00 AM

Morning Circle

Stories, songs, calendar activities, and group discussions begin the learning day.

10:00 AM

Learning Centers

Hands-on activities including literacy, math, science, art, and sensory play.

11:30 AM

Outdoor Play

Active play encourages physical development, teamwork, and exploration.

12:00 PM

Lunch & Rest Time

Healthy meals followed by quiet rest to recharge for the afternoon.

2:30 PM

Creative Activities

Music, crafts, dramatic play, and collaborative learning experiences.

4:30 PM

Wrap-Up & Pickup

Children reflect on their day before heading home with their families.

FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS

Have Questions? We're Here to Help.

Find answers to some of the most common questions families ask before enrolling.

What age groups do you serve?

We provide programs for infants, toddlers, preschool, Pre-K, and school-age children in a safe and nurturing environment.

How do I enroll my child?

Schedule a tour, complete the enrollment forms, and our team will guide you through the remaining steps.

What are your operating hours?

Please contact our center for current operating hours and availability.

Do you provide meals and snacks?

Nutritious meals and snacks may be available depending on the program. Please contact us for details.

Can I schedule a tour before enrolling?

Absolutely! We encourage families to visit our center, meet our teachers, and explore our classrooms before enrollment.

Ready to Find the Perfect Program?

We'd love to welcome your family. Schedule a tour and discover how Method Child Development Center helps children learn, grow, and thrive.
