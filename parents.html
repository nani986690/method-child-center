DAILY COMMUNICATION

Stay Connected Every Day

We believe strong communication between families and teachers creates the best learning experience for every child.

ProCare

ProCare provides real-time communication between teachers and families throughout the day.

  • Real-time classroom updates
  • Meal & snack tracking
  • Potty & diaper updates
  • Daily photos
  • Teacher messages
  • Individual child access code

Remind

Remind keeps families informed about important school announcements and upcoming events.

  • School closures
  • Important announcements
  • Holiday reminders
  • School events
  • Emergency notifications
  • Parent updates

METHOD PARENT ASSOCIATION

Family Involvement Group (FIG)

The Family Involvement Group (FIG) gives parents the opportunity to actively participate in the growth of Method Child Development Center while building a stronger community for every child.

Influence The Center

Each year, active parent members elect a representative to serve on the Board of Directors, helping shape the future direction of the Center.

Support School Events

Help organize educational programs, family events, seasonal celebrations, and staff appreciation activities.

Stay Connected

Build meaningful relationships with teachers, staff, and other families while staying informed about everything happening at the Center.

VOLUNTEER OPPORTUNITIES
