ABOUT THE BOARD

Dedicated Leaders Serving Our Community

Method Child Development Center is guided by a volunteer Board of Directors composed of parents, community leaders, and professionals who are passionate about supporting children, families, and early childhood education. Their leadership helps ensure strong governance, financial responsibility, and long-term sustainability for our organization.

Strong Governance

Providing responsible leadership and oversight to support our mission and long-term success.

Community Leadership

Representing families, educators, and community members while helping shape the future of our organization.

Accountability

Ensuring ethical decision-making, transparency, and responsible management of nonprofit resources.
